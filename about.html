About us

We are a group of Rutgers students composed of various majors branching off of the School of Engineering. We were given a goal to design and create a product that would satisfy a certain group of people in order to test our capabilities as students who yearn to improve our skills as a team among other things.

We based our project on the legendary childhood phenomenom known as Heelys. What makes our product so unique is the motorized functions which allow users to freely navigate the world using a remote connected to their Motorized Heelys. Without the need to use their legs to, users can leisurely roam about. Sounds fun, doesn't it?
